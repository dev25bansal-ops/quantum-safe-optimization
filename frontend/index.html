Quantum Approximate Optimization Algorithm

QAOA is a hybrid quantum-classical algorithm designed for combinatorial optimization problems. It uses parameterized quantum circuits to explore solution spaces that would be intractable classically.

  • Max-Cut, Graph Coloring, TSP
  • Configurable circuit depth (p-layers)
  • Classical optimizer integration
  • PennyLane & Qiskit backends

Variational Quantum Eigensolver

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)?

Post-Quantum Cryptography refers to cryptographic algorithms that are secure against attacks by both classical and quantum computers. Our platform uses ML-KEM and ML-DSA, which are standardized by NIST in FIPS 203 and FIPS 204.

Do I need my own quantum hardware access?

No! We provide access to IBM Quantum, AWS Braket, Azure Quantum, and D-Wave through our platform. You can also use our local simulator for development and testing at no cost.

What optimization problems can I solve?

You can solve any QUBO (Quadratic Unconstrained Binary Optimization) problem, including Max-Cut, TSP, Graph Coloring, Portfolio Optimization, and custom combinatorial problems.

How does the free tier work?

The free tier gives you 100 optimization jobs per month using our local simulator. It's perfect for learning, prototyping, and small experiments. Upgrade anytime to access real quantum hardware.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes! All data is encrypted using AES-256-GCM with keys exchanged via ML-KEM-768. Digital signatures use ML-DSA-65. This hybrid approach provides quantum-safe security for all your optimization data.

Can I deploy on-premise?

Yes, enterprise customers can deploy QuantumSafe Optimize on their own infrastructure. We provide Docker containers and Terraform configurations for Azure deployment.
